About Cluckstars

Cluckstars is the story of five local chickens who are always up to a new adventure which provides an opportunity for them to learn. It is a curriculum-based numeracy show that will keep our young learners engaged and help them sharpen their numeracy skills and other key skills such as listening, teamwork, leadership and respect.


Cluckstars take us through an adventure of knowledge and entertainment
